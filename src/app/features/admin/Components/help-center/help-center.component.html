Frequently Asked Questions

To reset a doctor's password:

  1. Go to Doctors Management
  2. Find the doctor in the list
  3. Click on "Edit"
  4. Scroll to the password section
  5. Click "Reset Password"
  6. The doctor will receive an email with password reset instructions

Bulk patient upload requires a CSV file with specific columns:

  • Download our template CSV file from the Patient Management page
  • Fill in patient data following the format
  • Go to Patient Management > Import
  • Upload your CSV file
  • Review the preview and confirm import

Content changes may not appear immediately due to:

  • Browser caching - try clearing your cache or using incognito mode
  • CDN caching - changes may take up to 15 minutes to propagate
  • If you still don't see changes, contact IT support to check for deployment issues

To create a custom report:

  1. Go to Report Management
  2. Select either Doctor or Patient reports
  3. Apply filters for your specific needs
  4. Click "Generate Report"
  5. Choose your export format (PDF, Excel, CSV)
  6. Save or print the report

If the system is slow, try these steps:

  • Refresh your browser
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Try a different browser (Chrome is recommended)
  • Check your internet connection
  • If the issue persists, contact IT support with details about what you were doing when the slowness occurred
